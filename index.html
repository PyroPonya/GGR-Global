Affiliate program
An affiliate management system is a software platform that businesses use to manage their affiliate programs. The system typically includes tools for tracking affiliate referrals, generating reports, managing commissions and payouts, and communicating with affiliates
Affiliate Dashboard
The system should provide an easy-to-use dashboard for affiliates to track their referrals, commissions, and payouts.
Tracking & Reporting
The system has robust tracking and reporting capabilities to enable businesses to monitor the performance of their affiliate program.
Marketing Materials
The system includes tools for providing affiliates with marketing materials such as banners, text links, and landing pages.
Commission Management
The system provides tools for managing commissions and payouts to affiliates, including the ability to set commission rates and payout thresholds.
Communication
The system includes tools for communicating with affiliates, including email marketing, push notifications, and in-app messaging.
Security & Compliance
The system is secure and comply with applicable laws and regulations related to data privacy, advertising, and marketing.
